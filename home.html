Welcome to the Genshin Impact World

Genshin Impact is a fun adventure RPG game made by miHoYo.
It takes place in a beautiful open world called :

  • Teyvat
  • full of nature
  • cities
  • unique characters each with their own special powers
Players explore, solve puzzles, fight enemies, and discover an exciting story.
